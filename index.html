About Me

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor at Jain University. I completed my PhD in the Department of AI at IIT Kharagpur. My research focuses on Trend Analysis and Predictive Modelling in Academic Genealogy Networks.

Notable platforms in this domain include the Mathematics Genealogy Project (MGP) and the Academic Family Tree (AFT).
