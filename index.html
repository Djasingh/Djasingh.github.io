About Me

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor at Jain University. I completed my PhD in the Department of AI at IIT Kharagpur. My current research focuses on Trend Analysis and Predictive Modelling in Academic Genealogy Networks. The academic genealogy network refers to a directed acyclic graph in which nodes refer to researchers and edegs refer to advisor-advisee relationships. Some of the widely recognized academic genealogy networks are the Mathematics Genealogy Project (MGP) and the Academic Family Tree (AFT).
